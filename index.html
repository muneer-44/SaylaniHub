Vision

To ensure that persons with disabilities (PWDs) have equal access to opportunities and are an integral part of society.

Mission

To promote an inclusive society through holistic and sustainable endeavours in the areas of engagement, empowerment and education.

Core Values

Inclusive

NOWPDP ensures that persons with disabilities are a pivotal part of mainstream society and in fact “A part. Not apart.”

Accessible

NOWPDP aims to discard attitudinal and infrastructural barriers in every field of life to ensure that persons with disabilities do not get hindered.

Sustainable

NOWPDP works on the societal model of disability providing reasonable, innovative and sustainable solutions for the empowerment of persons with disabilities for all time to come for a longer run.
